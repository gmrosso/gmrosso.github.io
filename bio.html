Welcome! I am a PhD Economics candidate at the University of Oxford where I am supervised by Prof. Andrea Ferrero.

My main research interests lie in the fields of international macroeconomics and macro-finance , particularly in understanding the drivers of dominant currencies and the role of financial intermediaries in the amplification and transmission of international spillovers.

I hold an Mphil in Economics from the University of Cambridge and a BSc in Philosophy and Logic from the London School of Economics and Political Science.
